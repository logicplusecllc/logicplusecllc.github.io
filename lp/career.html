Working With Us

We offer a range of exciting career opportunities, providing a rewarding work environment in which individuals can realize their full potential and help to shape the future of the petroleum sector.

Whether you're an experienced Professional, a talented Junior or a motivated Student, Logic Plus Electrical Contracting LLC offers you a variety of career opportunities that fits your ambitions. We are constantly looking for passionate individuals who want to demonstrate great work within a continuously challenging industry.

If you are enthusiastic about your work and ready to make impactful decisions, feel free to send us your CV. We are interested in hiring professionals for a long term. You can always submit a spontaneous application. By filling the application form, your application will be reviewed so you can be contacted for future job opportunities that fit your competencies.
